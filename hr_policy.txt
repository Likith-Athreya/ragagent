Remote Work Policy (2024)

1. Eligibility:
   - Employees must have 6+ months tenure
   - Role must be deemed remote-compatible by HR

2. Requirements:
   - Dedicated workspace with reliable internet (min 50Mbps)
   - Core hours: 10 AM - 3 PM local time for meetings

3. Equipment:
   - Company provides laptop + $500 stipend for peripherals
   - IT support available remotely M-F 8 AM - 6 PM

4. Expenses:
   - $50/month internet reimbursement
   - Coworking space access available upon approval

5. Office Visits:
   - Required quarterly for team-building (expenses covered)
